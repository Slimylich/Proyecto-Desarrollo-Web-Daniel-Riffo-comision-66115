Franquicias360

RAM Salud mental

For the web development of the page for RAM, I made an investigation based on the client needs and also the future objectives that the client had in mind, so with that in mind the study was focused in "how we can give a secure and competent service to our patients", from then on, the focus was on transparency and navigability of the webpage, making it easy for clients that ranged from 20 years old to 60 years old in all of latinoamerica.
